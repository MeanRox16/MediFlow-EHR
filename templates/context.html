EHR Clinical Context

This prototype is designed for a Cardiology and Radiology Specialty Clinic.

Medical Use Case

Doctors use this system to track patient demographics alongside high-resolution diagnostic imaging. The goal is to reduce the "diagnostic gap" by centralizing clinical notes and scans in one secure location.

Technological Scope
  • Doctor-specific data isolation.
  • Secure relational database storage.
  • Integrated Radiology Gallery (Multiple Scans).
